# Brand Guide: Whoopsy

## The Core
**Name:** Whoopsy
**Elevator Pitch:** "Creates games that rock with no bs!"
**Mission:** Create games devs want to make and players can enjoy without feeling dirty. Real, honest products born from passion.
**Values:** Fun + Real + Blunt.

## The Vibe
**Archetype:** The Wise Jester. Witty, Rebellious, Blunt.
**Voice:** Casual, Jesting, Peer-to-Peer. "Whatever we damn well feel like, you're not my boss!"
**Spectrum:**
- Serious vs Playful: Leans Playful (but grounded).
- Modern vs Classic: Very Modern.
- Exclusive vs Accessible: **Exclusive** (This is interesting - implies a "club" feel or high barrier to entry/quality?).
- Bold vs Subtle: **Very Bold**.

## Visual Direction
**Inspiration:** Team Fortress 2 (Goofy attitude, tight loop), Valve (Dynamic teams), Sucker Punch (Employee voice).
**Key Feelings:** Happy, Content, Alive.
**Aesthetic:** "Goofy attitude + Tight gameplay."

## Website Goals
1. Users find and use our games.
2. Devs want to join us.
